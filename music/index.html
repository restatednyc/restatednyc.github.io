MUSIC

With a wide-ranging repertoire and a powerhouse sound, Restated has earned awards on regional and national levels. We use only our voices to make our music—we use no drum tracks or other instruments. Lots of people have enjoyed listening to us, from the guys at the Mouth Off podcast to Perez Hilton. But don't take their word for it—hear for yourself!
