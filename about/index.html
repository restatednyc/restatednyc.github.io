About

Restated is one of New York City's premier a cappella groups, and prides itself on producing a powerful, audience-pleasing sound both in live performance and in the studio. The group was founded in 2007 as The Red States, but after consistently explaining to people that the group had No Political Affiliation (the name of its first album), decided a change was in order. Restated A Cappella was introduced to our fans, friends, and families at the group's 5-Year Anniversary show on October 10, 2012 at The Bitter End in New York City.
